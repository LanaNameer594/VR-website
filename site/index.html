Magical Forest

VR Experience

Magical Forest VR is a virtual reality experience that combines the latest technology with a strong stoy driven narrative. The experience invites participants to embark on a journey through a beautiful forest, with the premise inspiring action against climate change.

This innovative VR experience isn’t just about playing a game; it’s about creating a deep connection with nature.

This immersive world invites you to explore, feel, and act.

Discover a forest that responds to your presence
then take your experience beyond the headset into the real world.
