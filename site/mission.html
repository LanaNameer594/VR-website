Our Mission

Magical Forest VR goes beyond traditional gaming; it’s a transformative experience that blends innovation, storytelling, and interactivity to inspire a new generation. By immersing youth in a narrative that highlights the beauty of nature and the consequences of their actions, this VR experience fosters a sense of responsibility and encourages a proactive mindset toward environmental conservation.

Ultimately, Magical Forest VR is more than just an invitation to explore an enchanting world; it’s a call to action. It empowers participants to recognize their potential to make a difference and ignites a passion for climate action that can lead to real change. This immersive experience serves as a powerful reminder that even small actions can contribute to a brighter future for our planet, making it a vital tool for fostering environmental awareness among young people. Together, we can cultivate a sustainable future, one magical experience at a time.

Research & SDGs

We are currently researching youth engagement in this project through an AUS-funded study, aligning our work with the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Data has been collected from COP28 and the Sharjah Entrepreneurship Festival (SEFF), and we are now conducting a VR activation at the American University of Sharjah.

Over 200 participants have engaged with the immersive experience and completed a survey assessing engagement, behavioral change, and cognition. By addressing SDG goals related to quality education, climate action, and innovation, this research helps us refine our approach to effectively foster youth engagement in climate action through immersive storytelling and interactive experiences.
