The Storyline

The primary goal of Magical Forest VR is to engage young people through immersive storytelling that captures their imagination and inspires them to take action for the planet. The journey begins by inviting users to explore the forest, allowing them to connect with its beauty and feel a sense of presence. However, this magical world soon reveals a troubling reality: the forest is in danger of dying. Guided by the wise canary and other mystical creatures, players discover a mischievous goblin lurking in the shadows, wasting precious resources like electricity, water, and food.

The story reaches a powerful climax when the goblin disappears in the lake and the players catch a glimpse of their own reflection in the lake, only to see that they are mimicking the goblin’s actions.
